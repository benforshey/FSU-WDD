How many cups of coffee per year?

Directions

  • You'll be prompted 7 times (once per day of the week) to enter how many cups of coffee you had
  • If you didn't have an average number of cups, please enter what would be an average number of cups
  • You are given 7 prompts because you may have a pattern of drinking more or less on certain days
